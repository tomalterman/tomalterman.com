London Web Standards

London - July 2013

My talk at London Web Standards. We as developers are great at getting excited about ideas but can be bad at fleshing them out. That often leads to wasted effort or, worse, bad products. I show the power of sketching, how easy it is (regardless of drawing ability) and give a few tips on how to do it effectively to build great products quickly.
