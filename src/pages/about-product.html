Product Management

Whilst working with product-focussed tech startups I have learnt the importance of an effective product management.

While leading development and design teams, I have taken up more and more product management responsibilities to ensure that we are using our time effectively to tackle the most important business problems.

My experience includes:

  • Identifying the Minimum Viable Product
  • Managing Agile development projects
  • Identifying and gathering effective business metrics and KPIs
  • Writing effective user stories
  • Developing and validating product hypotheses
  • Data analysis
